Welcome to the Retro Blog

By Sina | Published on June 19, 2026 | Tags: #meta #retro

Welcome to my retro-styled blog! This is a green-screen aesthetic space for documenting security research, CEH learning, and penetration testing notes.

Why Retro?

Because the early internet was cooler than you remember. Green phosphor screens, minimal design, pure content. No tracking, no ads, no bloat.

What You'll Find Here

  • CEH certification progress and notes
  • Penetration testing lab writeups
  • Security research and findings
  • Tool reviews and configurations
  • Learning resources and guides

How to Add Your Own Posts

It's simple! Follow the guide in your README.md file.

# To add a new post: 1. Create post in posts/ folder 2. Add entry to posts.json 3. git push
